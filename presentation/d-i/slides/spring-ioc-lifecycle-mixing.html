Bean Life Cycle (4)

You can mix multiple lifecycle events

  • Initialization methods are called in this order:
    • methods annotated with @PostConstruct
    • afterPropertiesSet() method from InitializingBean
    • custom configured init()
  • Destroy methods are called in the same order:
    • methods annotated with @PreDestroy
    • destroy method from DisposableBean
    • custom configured destroy()
