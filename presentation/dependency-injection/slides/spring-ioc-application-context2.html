What does ApplicationContext do?

  • loads configuration metadata (filesystem, classpath, etc.)
  • it's an advanced factory: creates objects and wires them
  • maintains a registry of beans and their dependencies
  • allows registering of objects created outside the container

It is an advanced Factory that maintains a registry of beans and their dependencies.

Accepts resource strings that allow the container to load the configuration metadata from a variety of different external resources:

  • classpath
  • filesystem
  • etc
